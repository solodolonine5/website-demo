Understanding Surplus Funds

Surplus funds (also called "overage" or "excess funds") are created when a property is sold at foreclosure or tax auction for more than the amount owed. These excess proceeds legally belong to the former property owner or their heirs.

The funds we contact you about are held in county court registries following association foreclosures, mortgage foreclosures, or tax-deed foreclosure sales. Many homeowners are unaware they may be entitled to these funds.
